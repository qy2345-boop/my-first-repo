Spatial Canvases

A collection of 2D and 3D spatial studies exploring interaction, motion, material, image effects, and graphic systems through p5.js and Three.js.

3D Spatial Canvas 01

Rubik’s Cube study with animated layer rotation, orbit camera, and graphic materials.

3D Spatial Canvas 02

Clover flip field with glossy materials, color changes, and wave animation.

2D Spatial Canvas 01

Frosted cat field with pixel-glass masking and animated image movement.

2D Spatial Canvas 02

Interactive fan-cat scene: hold the mouse to power the fan and launch the cat.
