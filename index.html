Summary

This archive brings together a series of browser-based computational studies developed through Three.js, p5.js, and D3.js. Each entry records a distinct approach to motion, interaction, graphic behavior, and temporal data.

Number: 2026-TEMPORAL-03

Date: 2026/07/12

Type: D3.js / Temporal Structure

D3

When Do I Wake Up?

A temporal study of sleep interruptions and recurring wake patterns.

Apple Health sleep data is organized across nightly timelines and monthly calendars to examine how often awakenings occur, when they cluster, and which sleep stages usually precede them.

Date: 2026/07/19

Type: D3.js / Relational Structure

D3

Actors, Models, and Datasets

A relational map of the organizations, models, and datasets behind LAION-5B.

An interactive D3.js network examining how actors, machine-learning models, filtering operations, and datasets participate in the construction of LAION-5B. Nodes can be selected and repositioned to isolate their direct relationships.

Number: 2026-GEOSPATIAL-05

Date: 2026/07/22

Type: Mapbox / Geospatial Structure

MAP

Dark Kitchen Hunt

An interactive investigation of potential dark-kitchen clusters in Beijing.

Food-delivery platform listings collected from five observation points are mapped through store locations, density, sales, category, and chain affiliation. The project uses geographic exploration to compare visible storefronts with suspected hidden food-production spaces.
